About REIKAI Hardware

关于热开五金

Ningbo Fenghua REIKAI Hardware Factory is the upgraded successor of FengHua XINDA Tool Manufacturing Co., Ltd. (Est. 1996). With our professional R&D team and advanced technical capabilities, we have successfully transformed from traditional tool manufacturing to specialized high-end kitchen and furniture hardware production.

宁波奉化热开五金厂是奉化鑫达工具制造有限公司（成立于1996年）的全面升级版本。依托专业的技术研发团队和先进的制造能力，我们已成功从传统工具制造转型为高端厨房家具五金专业制造商。

We pride ourselves on providing custom solutions for listed companies and maintaining close technical partnerships with Taiwan enterprises. Our commitment to quality and innovation has made us a trusted partner for customers in 50+ countries.

我们为上市公司提供定制产品解决方案，并与台湾企业保持密切技术合作。凭借对品质和创新的追求，我们已成为50多个国家客户的信赖合作伙伴。
